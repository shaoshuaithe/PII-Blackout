# PII Blackout: Secure, AI-Powered Local PDF Redaction 🚀

**PII Blackout** is a professional, high-performance desktop application dedicated to **fully automated, 100% local PDF sensitive data redaction (blackout)**. 

If you handle confidential legal, medical, or financial documents, PII Blackout provides the ultimate balance between cutting-edge AI automation and absolute data security.

## 🎯 Core Pillars

### 1. 🤖 100% AUTOMATED AI REDACTION
No more manual, line-by-line searching. Our smart engine leverages advanced **GLiNER** and **Presidio AI** models to instantly scan, identify, and automatically mask sensitive information within seconds.
* **Auto-Detects:** Names, Email addresses, Phone numbers, Physical addresses, and more.
* **Custom Keywords:** Type any specific phrases or internal codes, and the engine will automatically find and black them out across the entire document.

### 2. 🔒 ZERO-CLOUD, 100% LOCAL PROCESSING
Your data sovereignty is our top priority. Built with a strict **"Privacy-First"** philosophy:
* **Completely Offline:** All AI inference and PDF processing run entirely on your local machine.
* **No Data Leakage:** Your confidential documents are **never** uploaded to the cloud, making it fully compliant with strict privacy regulations like **GDPR, HIPAA**, and corporate security policies.

### 3. 📄 SMART PDF BLACKOUT & MANUAL CONTROL
Get the perfect blend of automation and human oversight:
* **Precise Masking:** Securely burns black overlays into the PDF structure, ensuring redacted text cannot be uncovered or recovered.
* **One-Click Override:** The AI automatically highlights detected items, but you stay in full control. Easily add custom manual redaction boxes or cancel an AI-detected item with a single click.
